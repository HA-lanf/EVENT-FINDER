About GTTIS

GTTIS is an innovative app that helps users discover and explore events based on their interests. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, comedy lover, or business professional, GTTIS connects you to events happening nearby, making it easier to fuel your passions and expand your horizons.

Features

  • Personalized Recommendations
  • Event Categorization
  • Geolocation Services
  • Event Promotion for Organizers

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: React Native, Expo
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js, Firebase
  • APIs: Google Maps API, Eventbrite API (optional)
